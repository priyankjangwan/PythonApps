Correlation of Variables:
-1 : Negative Relationship (As one variable increases, the other variable decreases)
0 : No Relationship between the variables
1 : Positive Relationship (As one variable increases, the other variable increases)

Data Visualization
Types:
- Univariate
	- Study of one variable
		- Histogram, Bar, Boxplot
- Bivariate
	- Study of 2 variables
		- Line, Scatter
- Multivariate
	- Study of more than 2 variable
		- Pie, Heatmap
