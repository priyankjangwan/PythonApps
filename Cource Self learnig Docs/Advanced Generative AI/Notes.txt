ENC Training & Inference - Summarization

DEC Training - understands relationship

DEC Testing - predicts Marathi translations


Encoder

	Takes an input sequence/sentence.

	Processes each token using Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), LSTMs, GRUs, or Transformers.

	Outputs a fixed-size vector (context) summarizing the input.



Decoder

	Starts with the context vector from the encoder and Generates the output sequence one step at a time.
