Probability Distribution
	- Function that gives the probability of a different outcomes that can happen in a experiment

Types of Variables
	- Discrete (Rolling Dice, Coin Flip)
		- Binomial
			- Probability of getting 3 heads in 5 tosses
		- Bernoulli
			- Probability of getting a head when 1 coin is tossed
		- Poisson 
			- Probability of getting 10 emails in 1 hour
	- Continuous (Height, Weight, Temp)
		- Normal / Gaussian
			- Probability of a person having a particular height
		- Exponential
			- Time until your next bus arrives
		- Uniform
			- Probability of getting 5 in a random numbers
